About AutoDoc

AutoDoc is designed to provide gentle and empathetic guidance based on your symptoms. While it's not a replacement for professional medical care, it aims to give you a better understanding of what might be going on with your health.

What AutoDoc Does:

AutoDoc uses artificial intelligence and our research to assess symptoms and suggest potential causes based on a wide variety of health scenarios. It’s designed to help you feel supported and empowered in managing your health.

We aim to make sure that users are always encouraged to seek professional medical care if necessary. Dr. GPT serves as an informative guide, not a substitute for a doctor’s advice.
